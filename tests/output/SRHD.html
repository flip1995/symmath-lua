$\gamma$ = heat capacity ratio
$\rho$ = rest-mass density
${v^x}$ = 3-velocity
${e_{int}}$ = rest-mass internal specific energy
primitives:
${w}^i = {\left[\matrix{\rho\\{v^x}\\{e_{int}}}\right]}$
$W = {\frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - {{{v^x}}^{2}}}}}$ = Lorentz factor
${{{v^x}}^{2}} = {1 - {\frac{1}{{W}^{2}}}}$ = velocity in terms of Lorentz factor
$P$ = pressure
$P = {{{({\gamma - 1})} \rho} {e_{int}}}$ = pressure for an ideal gas
$h = {{1 + {e_{int}}} + {\frac{P}{\rho}}}$ = internal specific enthalpy
$h = {1 + {\gamma \cdot {e_{int}}}}$ = internal specific enthalpy for an ideal gas
${e_{int}} = {\frac{-{({1 - h})}}{\gamma}}$ = internal specific energy in terms of enthalpy
${e_{int}} = {\frac{-{({P + {\rho - {h \rho}}})}}{\rho}}$
$D = {\rho \cdot W}$ = density
${S^x}$ = momentum
$\tau$ = total energy density
conservatives:
${U}^i = {\left[\matrix{D\\{S^x}\\\tau}\right]}$
conservative definitions:
${U}^i = {\left[\matrix{{\rho \cdot W}\\{{{\rho \cdot h} {{W}^{2}}} {v^x}}\\{{{{\rho \cdot h} {{W}^{2}}} - P} - {\rho \cdot W}}}\right]}$
...in terms of primitives:
${U}^i = {\left[\matrix{{\rho \cdot {\frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - {{{v^x}}^{2}}}}}}\\{{{\rho \cdot {({{1 + {e_{int}}} + {\frac{{{({\gamma - 1})} \rho} {e_{int}}}{\rho}}})}} {{{({\frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - {{{v^x}}^{2}}}}})}}^{2}}} {v^x}}\\{{{{\rho \cdot {({{1 + {e_{int}}} + {\frac{{{({\gamma - 1})} \rho} {e_{int}}}{\rho}}})}} {{{({\frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - {{{v^x}}^{2}}}}})}}^{2}}} - {{{({\gamma - 1})} \rho} {e_{int}}}} - {\rho \cdot {\frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - {{{v^x}}^{2}}}}}}}}\right]}$
change in conservative vars wrt primitives:
${{\partial{U^i}}\over{\partial w^j}} = {\left[\matrix{W&{\rho \cdot {v^x} \cdot {{W}^{3}}}&0\\{{v^x} \cdot {{W}^{2}} h}&{\frac{-{\rho \cdot h {({1 - {2 {{W}^{2}}}})}}}{{2 - {{W}^{2}}} + {{{{v^x}}^{4}} {{W}^{2}}}}}&{\rho \cdot \gamma \cdot {v^x} \cdot {{W}^{2}}}\\{\frac{-{({1 + {{{{W \gamma} - \gamma} - h} - {h {{W}^{2}} \gamma}} + {h \gamma}})}}{\gamma}}&{\frac{-{\rho \cdot {v^x} \cdot W {({1 - {2 W h}})}}}{{2 - {{W}^{2}}} + {{{{v^x}}^{4}} {{W}^{2}}}}}&{\rho \cdot {({{1 - \gamma} + {\gamma \cdot {{W}^{2}}}})}}}\right]}$
flux in terms of conservative variables:
${F}^i = {\left[\matrix{{D {v^x}}\\{{{S^x} \cdot {v^x}} - P}\\{{S^x} - {D {v^x}}}}\right]}$

${F}^i = {\left[\matrix{{\frac{\rho \cdot {v^x}}{\sqrt{1 - {{{v^x}}^{2}}}}}\\{\frac{-{\rho \cdot {({{{{\gamma \cdot {e_{int}}} - {2 \gamma \cdot {e_{int}} \cdot {{{v^x}}^{2}}}} - {e_{int}}} + {{{e_{int}} \cdot {{{v^x}}^{2}}} - {{{v^x}}^{2}}}})}}}{1 - {{{v^x}}^{2}}}}\\{\frac{\rho \cdot {v^x} \cdot {({{1 - {\sqrt{1 - {{{v^x}}^{2}}}}} + {\gamma \cdot {e_{int}}}})}}{1 - {{{v^x}}^{2}}}}}\right]}$

${{\partial{F^i}}\over{\partial w^j}} = {\left[\matrix{{{v^x} \cdot W}&{\rho \cdot {{W}^{3}}}&0\\{\frac{-{({{{{1 - \gamma} - h} - {h {{W}^{2}} \gamma}} + {2 h \gamma}})}}{\gamma}}&{\frac{2 \rho \cdot {v^x} \cdot {{W}^{2}} h}{{2 - {{W}^{2}}} + {{{{v^x}}^{4}} {{W}^{2}}}}}&{\rho \cdot {({{1 - {2 \gamma}} + {\gamma \cdot {{W}^{2}}}})}}\\{-{{v^x} \cdot W {({1 - {W h}})}}}&{\frac{\rho \cdot {({{1 - {3 {{W}^{2}}}} + {{{W}^{4}} - {h W}} + {{2 h {{W}^{3}}} - {{{{v^x}}^{4}} {{W}^{4}}}}})}}{W {({{2 - {{W}^{2}}} + {{{W}^{2}} {{{v^x}}^{4}}}})}}}&{\rho \cdot {v^x} \cdot \gamma \cdot {{W}^{2}}}}\right]}$
