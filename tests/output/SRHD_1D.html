$\gamma$ = heat capacity ratio
$\rho$ = rest-mass density
${v^x}$ = 3-velocity
${e_{int}}$ = rest-mass internal specific energy
primitives:
${{ w}^i} = {\left[\begin{matrix} \rho \\ {v^x} \\ {e_{int}}\end{matrix}\right]}$
${W} = {\frac{1}{\sqrt{{1} - {{{v^x}}^{2}}}}}$ = Lorentz factor
${{{v^x}}^{2}} = {{1} - {\frac{1}{{W}^{2}}}}$ = velocity in terms of Lorentz factor
$P$ = pressure
${P} = {{{{{({{\gamma} - {1}})}} {{\rho}}}} {{{e_{int}}}}}$ = pressure for an ideal gas
${h} = {{{1} + {{e_{int}}}} + {{\frac{1}{\rho}}{({P})}}}$ = internal specific enthalpy
${h} = {{1} + {{{\gamma}} \cdot {{{e_{int}}}}}}$ = internal specific enthalpy for an ideal gas
${{e_{int}}} = {{\frac{1}{\gamma}}{({-{({{1} - {h}})}})}}$ = internal specific energy in terms of enthalpy
${{e_{int}}} = {{\frac{1}{\rho}}{({-{({{P} + {{\rho} - {{{h}} {{\rho}}}}})}})}}$
${D} = {{{\rho}} \cdot {{W}}}$ = density
${S^x}$ = momentum
$\tau$ = total energy density
conservatives:
${{ U}^i} = {\left[\begin{matrix} D \\ {S^x} \\ \tau\end{matrix}\right]}$
conservative definitions:
${{ U}^i} = {\left[\begin{matrix} {{\rho}} \cdot {{W}} \\ {{{{{{\rho}} \cdot {{h}}}} {{{W}^{2}}}}} {{{v^x}}} \\ {{{{{{\rho}} \cdot {{h}}}} {{{W}^{2}}}} - {P}} - {{{\rho}} \cdot {{W}}}\end{matrix}\right]}$
...in terms of primitives:
${{ U}^i} = {\left[\begin{matrix} {{\rho}} \cdot {{\frac{1}{\sqrt{{1} - {{{v^x}}^{2}}}}}} \\ {{{{{{\rho}} \cdot {{({{{1} + {{e_{int}}}} + {{\frac{1}{\rho}}{({{{{{({{\gamma} - {1}})}} {{\rho}}}} {{{e_{int}}}}})}}})}}}} {{{({\frac{1}{\sqrt{{1} - {{{v^x}}^{2}}}}})}^{2}}}}} {{{v^x}}} \\ {{{{{{\rho}} \cdot {{({{{1} + {{e_{int}}}} + {{\frac{1}{\rho}}{({{{{{({{\gamma} - {1}})}} {{\rho}}}} {{{e_{int}}}}})}}})}}}} {{{({\frac{1}{\sqrt{{1} - {{{v^x}}^{2}}}}})}^{2}}}} - {{{{{({{\gamma} - {1}})}} {{\rho}}}} {{{e_{int}}}}}} - {{{\rho}} \cdot {{\frac{1}{\sqrt{{1} - {{{v^x}}^{2}}}}}}}\end{matrix}\right]}$

${{ F}^i} = {\left[\begin{matrix} {{D}} {{{v^x}}} \\ {{{{S^x}}} \cdot {{{v^x}}}} - {P} \\ {{S^x}} - {{{D}} {{{v^x}}}}\end{matrix}\right]}$

${{ F}^i} = {\left[\begin{matrix} \frac{{{{v^x}}} \cdot {{\rho}}}{\sqrt{{1} - {{{v^x}}^{2}}}} \\ \frac{-{{{\rho}} \cdot {{({{{{{{\gamma}} \cdot {{{e_{int}}}}} - {{{2}} {{\gamma}} \cdot {{{e_{int}}}} \cdot {{{{v^x}}^{2}}}}} - {{e_{int}}}} + {{{{{e_{int}}}} \cdot {{{{v^x}}^{2}}}} - {{{v^x}}^{2}}}})}}}}{{1} - {{{v^x}}^{2}}} \\ \frac{{{\rho}} \cdot {{{v^x}}} \cdot {{({{{1} - {\sqrt{{1} - {{{v^x}}^{2}}}}} + {{{\gamma}} \cdot {{{e_{int}}}}}})}}}{{1} - {{{v^x}}^{2}}}\end{matrix}\right]}$
