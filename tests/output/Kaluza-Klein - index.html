Kaluza-Klein with constant scalar field

unit coordinate convention: $dx^0 = c dt$

$1 = c =$ speed of light
$1 = G =$ gravitational constant
$1 = k_e =$ Coulomb's constant


${ A}_u$ = electromagnetic four-potential, in units $\frac{{{kg}} \cdot {{m}}}{{{C}} {{s}}}$
${{ A}_5} = {1}$ in natural units, but to cancel the units of $\phi$ it is in units of $\frac{{{kg}} \cdot {{m}}}{{{C}} {{s}}}$

$\phi$ = scalar field, in units $\frac{{{C}} {{s}}}{{{kg}} \cdot {{m}}}$

${{ g}_{\mu}}_{\nu}$ = 4D metric tensor, with units 1
${{ g}^{\mu}}^{\nu}$ = 4D metric inverse

${{ \tilde{g}}_u}_v$ = 5D metric tensor
${{{ \tilde{g}}_a}_b} = {\overset{a\downarrow b\rightarrow}{\left[\begin{matrix} {{ \tilde{g}}_{\alpha}}_{\beta} & {{ \tilde{g}}_{\alpha}}_5 \\ {{ \tilde{g}}_5}_{\beta} & {{ \tilde{g}}_5}_5\end{matrix}\right]}}$
${{{ \tilde{g}}_a}_b} = {\overset{a\downarrow b\rightarrow}{\left[\begin{matrix} {{{ g}_{\alpha}}_{\beta}} + {{{{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\alpha}}}}} {{{ A}_{\beta}}}} & {{{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\alpha}}}}} {{{ A}_5}} \\ {{{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\beta}}}}} {{{ A}_5}} & {{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{{ A}_5}^{2}}}\end{matrix}\right]}}$
Notice that I included the $A_5$ terms to show the units align. From here on I will omit them since their value is 1.
${{{ \tilde{g}}_a}_b} = {\overset{a\downarrow b\rightarrow}{\left[\begin{matrix} {{{ g}_{\alpha}}_{\beta}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\beta}}} {{{ A}_{\alpha}}}} & {{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\alpha}}} \\ {{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\beta}}} & {\phi}^{2}\end{matrix}\right]}}$

${{ \tilde{g}}^u}^v$ = 5D metric inverse
${{{ \tilde{g}}^a}^b} = {\overset{a\downarrow b\rightarrow}{\left[\begin{matrix} {{ g}^{\alpha}}^{\beta} & {-{{{ g}^{\alpha}}^{\mu}}} {{{ A}_{\mu}}} \\ {-{{{ g}^{\beta}}^{\mu}}} {{{ A}_{\mu}}} & {{{{{{{ g}^{\mu}}^{\nu}}} {{{ A}_{\mu}}}}} {{{ A}_{\nu}}}} + {\frac{1}{{\phi}^{2}}}\end{matrix}\right]}}$
Notice, if you see a raised 4-index, it is being raised by the 4-metric and not the 5-metric.
${{{ \tilde{g}}^a}^b} = {\overset{a\downarrow b\rightarrow}{\left[\begin{matrix} {{ g}^{\alpha}}^{\beta} & -{{ A}^{\alpha}} \\ -{{ A}^{\beta}} & {{{{ A}_{\mu}}} {{{ A}^{\mu}}}} + {\frac{1}{{\phi}^{2}}}\end{matrix}\right]}}$

The cylindrical constraint is
${{{{ \tilde{g}}_a}_b}_{,5}} = {0}$
Therefore
${\left[\begin{matrix} {{{{ g}_{\alpha}}_{\beta}}_{,5}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\alpha}}} {{{{ A}_{\beta}}_{,5}}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\beta}}} {{{{ A}_{\alpha}}_{,5}}}} + {{{2}} {{\phi}} \cdot {{{ A}_{\beta}}} {{{ \phi}_{,5}}} {{{ A}_{\alpha}}}} & {{\phi}} \cdot {{({{{{\phi}} \cdot {{{{ A}_{\alpha}}_{,5}}}} + {{{2}} {{{ \phi}_{,5}}} {{{ A}_{\alpha}}}}})}} \\ {{\phi}} \cdot {{({{{{\phi}} \cdot {{{{ A}_{\beta}}_{,5}}}} + {{{2}} {{{ A}_{\beta}}} {{{ \phi}_{,5}}}}})}} & {{2}} {{\phi}} \cdot {{{ \phi}_{,5}}}\end{matrix}\right]} = {0}$
Therefore
${{ \phi}_{,5}} = {0}$
${{{ A}_{\mu}}_{,5}} = {0}$
${{{{ g}_{\alpha}}_{\beta}}_{,5}} = {0}$

For now I'll use a constant scalar as well
${{ \phi}_{,a}} = {0}$

metric partial:
${{{{ \tilde{g}}_a}_b}_{,c}} = {\overset{a\downarrow b\rightarrow}{\left[\begin{matrix} {{{{ g}_{\alpha}}_{\beta}}_{,c}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\alpha}}} {{{{ A}_{\beta}}_{,c}}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\beta}}} {{{{ A}_{\alpha}}_{,c}}}} & {{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{{ A}_{\alpha}}_{,c}}} \\ {{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{{ A}_{\beta}}_{,c}}} & 0\end{matrix}\right]}}$
${{{{ \tilde{g}}_a}_b}_{,c}} = {\overset{c\downarrow[{a\downarrow b\rightarrow}]}{\left[\begin{matrix} \overset{a\downarrow b\rightarrow}{\left[\begin{matrix} {{{{ g}_{\alpha}}_{\beta}}_{,\gamma}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\alpha}}} {{{{ A}_{\beta}}_{,\gamma}}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\beta}}} {{{{ A}_{\alpha}}_{,\gamma}}}} & {{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{{ A}_{\alpha}}_{,\gamma}}} \\ {{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{{ A}_{\beta}}_{,\gamma}}} & 0\end{matrix}\right]} \\ \overset{a\downarrow b\rightarrow}{\left[\begin{matrix} 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0\end{matrix}\right]}\end{matrix}\right]}}$

lower connection
${{{{ \tilde{\Gamma}}_a}_b}_c} = {\overset{a\downarrow[{b\downarrow c\rightarrow}]}{\left[\begin{matrix} \overset{b\downarrow c\rightarrow}{\left[\begin{matrix} {\frac{1}{2}}{({{{{{ g}_{\alpha}}_{\beta}}_{,\gamma}} + {{{{{ g}_{\alpha}}_{\gamma}}_{,\beta}} - {{{{ g}_{\beta}}_{\gamma}}_{,\alpha}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\alpha}}} {{{{ A}_{\beta}}_{,\gamma}}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\alpha}}} {{{{ A}_{\gamma}}_{,\beta}}}} + {{{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\beta}}} {{{{ A}_{\alpha}}_{,\gamma}}}} - {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\beta}}} {{{{ A}_{\gamma}}_{,\alpha}}}}} + {{{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\gamma}}} {{{{ A}_{\alpha}}_{,\beta}}}} - {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\gamma}}} {{{{ A}_{\beta}}_{,\alpha}}}}}})} & {\frac{1}{2}}{({{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{({{{{ A}_{\alpha}}_{,\beta}} - {{{ A}_{\beta}}_{,\alpha}}})}}})} \\ {\frac{1}{2}}{({{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{({{{{ A}_{\alpha}}_{,\gamma}} - {{{ A}_{\gamma}}_{,\alpha}}})}}})} & 0\end{matrix}\right]} \\ \overset{b\downarrow c\rightarrow}{\left[\begin{matrix} {\frac{1}{2}}{({{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{({{{{ A}_{\beta}}_{,\gamma}} + {{{ A}_{\gamma}}_{,\beta}}})}}})} & 0 \\ 0 & 0\end{matrix}\right]}\end{matrix}\right]}}$
${{{{ \tilde{\Gamma}}_a}_b}_c} = {\overset{a\downarrow[{b\downarrow c\rightarrow}]}{\left[\begin{matrix} \overset{b\downarrow c\rightarrow}{\left[\begin{matrix} {\frac{1}{2}}{({{{{2}} {{{{{ \Gamma}_{\alpha}}_{\beta}}_{\gamma}}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\alpha}}} {{{{ A}_{\beta}}_{,\gamma}}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\alpha}}} {{{{ A}_{\gamma}}_{,\beta}}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\beta}}} {{{{ F}_{\gamma}}_{\alpha}}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\gamma}}} {{{{ F}_{\beta}}_{\alpha}}}}})} & {\frac{1}{2}}{({{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{{ F}_{\beta}}_{\alpha}}}})} \\ {\frac{1}{2}}{({{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{{ F}_{\gamma}}_{\alpha}}}})} & 0\end{matrix}\right]} \\ \overset{b\downarrow c\rightarrow}{\left[\begin{matrix} {\frac{1}{2}}{({{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{({{{{ A}_{\beta}}_{,\gamma}} + {{{ A}_{\gamma}}_{,\beta}}})}}})} & 0 \\ 0 & 0\end{matrix}\right]}\end{matrix}\right]}}$

upper connection
${{{{ \tilde{\Gamma}}^a}_b}_c} = {{{{{ \overset{a\downarrow b\rightarrow}{\left[\begin{matrix} {{ g}^{\alpha}}^{\epsilon} & -{{ A}^{\alpha}} \\ -{{ A}^{\epsilon}} & {{{{ A}_{\mu}}} {{{ A}^{\mu}}}} + {\frac{1}{{\phi}^{2}}}\end{matrix}\right]}}^a}^e}} {{{{{ \overset{a\downarrow[{b\downarrow c\rightarrow}]}{\left[\begin{matrix} \overset{b\downarrow c\rightarrow}{\left[\begin{matrix} {\frac{1}{2}}{({{{{2}} {{{{{ \Gamma}_{\epsilon}}_{\beta}}_{\gamma}}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\epsilon}}} {{{{ A}_{\beta}}_{,\gamma}}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\epsilon}}} {{{{ A}_{\gamma}}_{,\beta}}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\beta}}} {{{{ F}_{\gamma}}_{\epsilon}}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\gamma}}} {{{{ F}_{\beta}}_{\epsilon}}}}})} & {\frac{1}{2}}{({{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{{ F}_{\beta}}_{\epsilon}}}})} \\ {\frac{1}{2}}{({{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{{ F}_{\gamma}}_{\epsilon}}}})} & 0\end{matrix}\right]} \\ \overset{b\downarrow c\rightarrow}{\left[\begin{matrix} {\frac{1}{2}}{({{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{({{{{ A}_{\beta}}_{,\gamma}} + {{{ A}_{\gamma}}_{,\beta}}})}}})} & 0 \\ 0 & 0\end{matrix}\right]}\end{matrix}\right]}}_e}_b}_c}}}$
${{{{ \tilde{\Gamma}}^a}_b}_c} = {\overset{a\downarrow[{b\downarrow c\rightarrow}]}{\left[\begin{matrix} \left[\begin{matrix} {\frac{1}{2}}{({{{{{{2}} {{{{ g}^{\alpha}}^{\epsilon}}} {{{{{ \Gamma}_{\epsilon}}_{\beta}}_{\gamma}}}} - {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}^{\alpha}}} {{{{ A}_{\beta}}_{,\gamma}}}}} - {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}^{\alpha}}} {{{{ A}_{\gamma}}_{,\beta}}}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\beta}}} {{{{ F}_{\gamma}}_{\epsilon}}} {{{{ g}^{\alpha}}^{\epsilon}}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\epsilon}}} {{{{ A}_{\beta}}_{,\gamma}}} {{{{ g}^{\alpha}}^{\epsilon}}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\epsilon}}} {{{{ A}_{\gamma}}_{,\beta}}} {{{{ g}^{\alpha}}^{\epsilon}}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\gamma}}} {{{{ F}_{\beta}}_{\epsilon}}} {{{{ g}^{\alpha}}^{\epsilon}}}}})} & {\frac{1}{2}}{({{{{{ g}^{\alpha}}^{\epsilon}}} {{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{{ F}_{\beta}}_{\epsilon}}}})} \\ {\frac{1}{2}}{({{{{{ g}^{\alpha}}^{\epsilon}}} {{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{{ F}_{\gamma}}_{\epsilon}}}})} & 0\end{matrix}\right] \\ \left[\begin{matrix} {\frac{1}{2}}{({-{({{{{{{2}} {{{ A}^{\epsilon}}} {{{{{ \Gamma}_{\epsilon}}_{\beta}}_{\gamma}}}} - {{{ A}_{\beta}}_{,\gamma}}} - {{{ A}_{\gamma}}_{,\beta}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}^{\epsilon}}} {{{ A}_{\epsilon}}} {{{{ A}_{\beta}}_{,\gamma}}}} + {{{{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}^{\epsilon}}} {{{ A}_{\epsilon}}} {{{{ A}_{\gamma}}_{,\beta}}}} - {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}^{\mu}}} {{{ A}_{\mu}}} {{{{ A}_{\beta}}_{,\gamma}}}}} - {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}^{\mu}}} {{{ A}_{\mu}}} {{{{ A}_{\gamma}}_{,\beta}}}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\beta}}} {{{ A}^{\epsilon}}} {{{{ F}_{\gamma}}_{\epsilon}}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\gamma}}} {{{ A}^{\epsilon}}} {{{{ F}_{\beta}}_{\epsilon}}}}})}})} & {\frac{1}{2}}{({-{{{{ A}^{\epsilon}}} {{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{{ F}_{\beta}}_{\epsilon}}}}})} \\ {\frac{1}{2}}{({-{{{{ A}^{\epsilon}}} {{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{{ F}_{\gamma}}_{\epsilon}}}}})} & 0\end{matrix}\right]\end{matrix}\right]}}$
${{{{ \tilde{\Gamma}}^a}_b}_c} = {\overset{a\downarrow[{b\downarrow c\rightarrow}]}{\left[\begin{matrix} \left[\begin{matrix} {\frac{1}{2}}{({{{{2}} {{{{{ \Gamma}^{\alpha}}_{\beta}}_{\gamma}}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\beta}}} {{{{ F}_{\gamma}}^{\alpha}}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\gamma}}} {{{{ F}_{\beta}}^{\alpha}}}}})} & {\frac{1}{2}}{({{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{{ F}_{\beta}}^{\alpha}}}})} \\ {\frac{1}{2}}{({{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{{ F}_{\gamma}}^{\alpha}}}})} & 0\end{matrix}\right] \\ \left[\begin{matrix} {\frac{1}{2}}{({-{({{{{{{2}} {{{ A}^{\epsilon}}} {{{{{ \Gamma}_{\epsilon}}_{\beta}}_{\gamma}}}} - {{{ A}_{\beta}}_{,\gamma}}} - {{{ A}_{\gamma}}_{,\beta}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\beta}}} {{{ A}^{\epsilon}}} {{{{ F}_{\gamma}}_{\epsilon}}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\gamma}}} {{{ A}^{\epsilon}}} {{{{ F}_{\beta}}_{\epsilon}}}}})}})} & {\frac{1}{2}}{({-{{{{ A}^{\epsilon}}} {{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{{ F}_{\beta}}_{\epsilon}}}}})} \\ {\frac{1}{2}}{({-{{{{ A}^{\epsilon}}} {{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{{ F}_{\gamma}}_{\epsilon}}}}})} & 0\end{matrix}\right]\end{matrix}\right]}}$

geodesic
${{{ \ddot{x}}^a} + {{{{{{{{ \tilde{\Gamma}}^a}_b}_c}} {{{ \dot{x}}^b}}}} {{{ \dot{x}}^c}}}} = {0}$
${{{ \ddot{x}}^a} + {{{{{ \dot{x}}^5}^{2}}} {{{{{ \tilde{\Gamma}}^{\alpha}}_5}_5}}} + {{{2}} {{{ \dot{x}}^5}} {{{ \dot{x}}^{\beta}}} {{{{{ \tilde{\Gamma}}^{\alpha}}_{\beta}}_5}}} + {{{{ \dot{x}}^{\beta}}} {{{ \dot{x}}^{\gamma}}} {{{{{ \tilde{\Gamma}}^{\alpha}}_{\beta}}_{\gamma}}}}} = {0}$
${{{ \ddot{x}}^a} + {{{{ \dot{x}}^{\beta}}} {{{ \dot{x}}^{\gamma}}} {{{{{ \Gamma}^{\alpha}}_{\beta}}_{\gamma}}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\beta}}} {{{ \dot{x}}^{\beta}}} {{{ \dot{x}}^{\gamma}}} {{{{ F}_{\gamma}}^{\alpha}}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ \dot{x}}^5}} {{{ \dot{x}}^{\beta}}} {{{{ F}_{\beta}}^{\alpha}}}}} = {0}$
Let $\dot{x}^5 = \frac{q}{m}$
${{{ \ddot{x}}^a} + {{{{ \dot{x}}^{\beta}}} {{{ \dot{x}}^{\gamma}}} {{{{{ \Gamma}^{\alpha}}_{\beta}}_{\gamma}}}} + {{{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ A}_{\beta}}} {{{ \dot{x}}^{\beta}}} {{{ \dot{x}}^{\gamma}}} {{{{ F}_{\gamma}}^{\alpha}}}} + {{{\frac{q}{m}}} \cdot {{{\phi}^{2}}} {{{ \dot{x}}^{\beta}}} {{{{ F}_{\beta}}^{\alpha}}}}} = {0}$

There you have gravitational force, Lorentz force, and some extra terms.
